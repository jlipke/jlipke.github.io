About Me

Lipke, Joe

I am a graduate of Fox Valley Technical College with my associate's in Software Development. Along the way, I have had the privilege to work with ASP.Net, MVC, Entity Framework, Javascript/JQuery, Xamarin, Swift and PHP while working with a team of peers.


Fox Valley Metrology performs in house and out call calibration services for a large variety of manufacturing companies. With this, they provide a customer website to allow customer employees multiple accounts with varying permissions to retrieve/store customer's calibration certifications, calibration reminders, and create new orders while remaining reliable for equipment auditing purposes.

Using either Azure DevOps or IT Tickets, I recreated and solved issues focused on the .Net/C# MVC website and a WinForms C#/VB desktop app. The website ran multiple API methods with t-sql/linq for retreiving/updating Customer calibration and Employee data. Also allowing access to Microsoft Outlook and Google Place API's, and SAP Crystal Reports as certifications.

Before I started focusing mainly in website issues, during the first year I was instructed to research and build a Xamarin/C# mobile app designed for employees to simplify communications for out call services and management. Utilizing MVVM structure, and various API methods to the website, retrieving similar data with the additions of allowing employees to scan labels, check in per diem receipts, and receive live job calendar updates. It was designed for iOS/Android, with low resource devices as the focus.

In my free time, I love to produce music, play video games, and enjoy the outdoors.
